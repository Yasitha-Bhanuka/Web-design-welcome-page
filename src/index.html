Birthday Cake
A birthday cake is a celebratory dessert specifically made to mark someone's birthday. It is typically a sweet and flavorful confection consisting of layers of cake filled with delicious frosting or cream. The cake is often covered in decorative icing and may feature personalized messages, sprinkles, or other edible embellishments. Candles are commonly placed on top of the cake, symbolizing the age of the person celebrating their birthday.

Cup Cake
A cupcake is a small, individual-sized cake that is baked in a cup-shaped container, typically made of paper or aluminum. It is a popular dessert that is by people of all ages. Cupcakes are known for their portability and convenience, as they can be easily held and eaten without the need for utensils. They come in various flavors and are often topped with frosting, sprinkles, or other decorative elements to add visual appeal.

Wedding Cake
A wedding cake is a special cake created and displayed at wedding receptions to celebrate the union of a couple. It is typically a multi-tiered cake, symbolizing the grandeur and significance of the occasion. Wedding cakes are meticulously designed and decorated to match the theme, colors, and style of the wedding. They are often adorned with intricate icing designs, edible flowers, or other decorative elements that reflect the couple's preferences and personalization.
